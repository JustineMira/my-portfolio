Objective

Create a clean, conversion-friendly Shopify product listing with accurate product data, consistent formatting, organized tags/collections, and completed SEO fields.

What I Did

  • Prepared product details in a sheet (title, price, vendor, product type, tags).
  • Created the product in Shopify Admin and formatted the description (benefits + specs).
  • Added collections and tags using a consistent naming system.
  • Uploaded images, arranged image order, and checked display on product page.
  • Filled SEO fields (meta title + meta description) and did a final QA pass.
